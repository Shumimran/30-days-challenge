
# Short Note: What is SPECKit Plus?

**SPECKit Plus** is a simple learning guide that teaches beginners how to build software in an organized and professional way.

It breaks the whole development process into 5 clear steps, so students know what to do first, next, and last.

## These 5 core concepts are:

**1️- /constitution** – Defines the rules, purpose, and goals of your project.

**2️. /specify** – Write clear instructions (specifications) about what your software should do.

**3️. /plan** – Break the work into simple steps and decide how you will build it.

**4️. /tasks** – Convert the plan into small, doable tasks you can complete one by one.

**5️. /implement** – Start building the actual software by following your tasks and plan.

## In short:

SPECKit Plus helps beginners create clean, structured, and error-free software by following a step-by-step method.
